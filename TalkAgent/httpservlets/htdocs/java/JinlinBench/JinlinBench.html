This applet is originally for Jinlin's birthday, I change it to a bench mark tool after I got my P2 400 (that is a year ago. It is not fast now...). It mainly test the row CPU power and 2d performance (the result will be affected by the combination of CPU power and 2d performance).

Current results:
  • Pentium II 450, nVidia TNT 16mg, 128Mg (Dell XPS 450, my new office PC): 4743 ms/loop
  • Pentium II 400 with nVidia 128, 128M (Home PC :-): 8364 ms/loop
  • Pentium 100 with ATI Pro Turbo PCI, 32M (my former office PC): 122540 ms/loop
